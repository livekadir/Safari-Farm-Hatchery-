🥚 Hatching Chart?

Bater: 18 Din | Murgi: 21 Din | Turkey: 28 Din. Temp 37.5°C se 37.8°C maintain karein.

Expert Tips 🩺

Brooding Care

Pehle 7 din chicks ke liye sabse zaroori hote hain. Brooder ka temperature 33°C-35°C hona chahiye. Niche saaf burda bichhayein. Pehle paani mein thoda jaggery (gur) aur anti-stress medicine mila kar dein.
